Crown Hall

Is it weird that the first project by Mies that I made in LEGO is a building that I haven't seen in person? By that point I had seen and fallen in love with the Lake Shore apartments, 330 North Wabash and the Neue Nationalgalerie, but I chose this crown jewel of IIT's campus, where I had never been. The thing has beautiful proportions. I'd like to see it some day.
