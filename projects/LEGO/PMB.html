Pembroke College, Oxford

Spending time at Oxford was a tremendous privilege, and I loved every second of it. I felt nostalgic for my college and all the people I met at the University, so I recreated it in LEGO. Notable alumni include Samuel Johnson and Walter Isaacson. JRR Tolkien and Roger Bannister are former fellow and Master, respectively. There is not much notable about the architecture, particularly when compared with the likes of Christ Church, Keble and All Souls, but I called it home for eight months and it is special to me. I have a lot of happy memories from this place tucked behind St. Aldate's Church.
